Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Derma Vision's skin disease detection system.

General Questions

What is Derma Vision?
Derma Vision is an AI-powered skin disease detection system that helps identify potential skin conditions through image analysis. It uses advanced machine learning algorithms to provide preliminary assessments of skin conditions.
How accurate is the detection system?
Our system achieves a high accuracy rate in identifying common skin conditions. However, it's important to note that this tool is meant for preliminary screening only and should not replace professional medical advice.
Is this a replacement for seeing a doctor?
No, Derma Vision is not a replacement for professional medical advice. It's designed as a preliminary screening tool to help you better understand potential skin conditions. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Technical Questions

What image formats are supported?
We support common image formats including JPG, PNG, and JPEG. The maximum file size is 16MB, and we recommend images with a minimum resolution of 500x500 pixels for best results.
How should I take photos for best results?
For best results:
  • Use good lighting (natural daylight is best)
  • Keep the camera steady and in focus
  • Include some surrounding healthy skin for context
  • Take multiple angles if possible
  • Avoid using flash photography
