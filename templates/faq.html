General Questions

What is Derma Vision?
Derma Vision is an AI-powered skin disease detection system that helps identify potential skin conditions through image analysis. It uses advanced machine learning algorithms to provide preliminary assessments of skin conditions.
How accurate is the detection system?
Our system achieves a high accuracy rate in identifying common skin conditions. However, it's important to note that this tool is meant for preliminary screening only and should not replace professional medical advice.
Is this a replacement for seeing a doctor?
No, Derma Vision is not a replacement for professional medical advice. It's designed as a preliminary screening tool to help you better understand potential skin conditions. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
Can I use Derma Vision on my phone?
Yes, Derma Vision is mobile-friendly and can be accessed from any modern smartphone or tablet with an internet connection.
What languages are supported?
Currently, Derma Vision supports English. We plan to add more languages in the future.

Technical Questions

What image formats are supported?
We support common image formats including JPG, PNG, and JPEG. The maximum file size is 16MB, and we recommend images with a minimum resolution of 500x500 pixels for best results.
How should I take photos for best results?
For best results:
  • Use good lighting (natural daylight is best)
  • Keep the camera steady and in focus
  • Include some surrounding healthy skin for context
  • Take multiple angles if possible
  • Avoid using flash photography

Accuracy Questions

What is the accuracy rate of Derma Vision?
Derma Vision achieves a high accuracy rate for common skin conditions, but accuracy may vary depending on image quality and condition type. Always consult a healthcare professional for a final diagnosis.
Can the system misidentify conditions?
Yes, as with any AI-based tool, there is a possibility of misidentification. The system is intended for preliminary screening and should not replace professional medical advice.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe?
We take your privacy seriously. All uploaded images are processed securely and are not shared with third parties.
How is my privacy protected?
We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during upload and processing. Your images and information are never shared with third parties and are deleted after analysis.
Who can access my uploaded images?
Only authorized system processes can access your uploaded images for the purpose of analysis. No human staff reviews your images unless you explicitly request support.
Are my images stored permanently?
No, all uploaded images are automatically deleted from our servers after the analysis is complete to ensure your privacy and security.
